About Hot Hot Fruit

Hot Hot Fruit is a fruit-themed slot by Habanero that blends classic BAR symbols, fruits, and red sevens with a more action-driven bonus structure.

The game uses a 5x3 layout with 15 fixed paylines, so the pace stays clear and easy to follow from the very first spin. Because of that, the game feels easy to understand but much more dynamic than a standard retro fruit machine. Hot Hot Fruit works especially well for players who enjoy classic visuals with bonus moments that can suddenly change the whole session.
